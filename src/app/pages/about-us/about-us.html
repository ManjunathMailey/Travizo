Our Story

A passion for the Western Ghats became a mission to share its magic with the world

Born from a Love for the
Western Ghats

TRAVIZO started as a weekend hiking blog in 2018 — a place where a group of friends from Bengaluru documented their adventures across the misty peaks of Munnar, the coffee-scented valleys of Coorg, and the silent backwaters of Alleppey.

What began as personal passion quickly became something more. Readers started writing in, asking for help planning their own trips. We realised that South India's most extraordinary experiences were hidden behind logistical complexity — the language barriers, the off-road routes, the seasonal windows that guidebooks never mention.

So we built TRAVIZO to solve exactly that. A team of local experts, sustainable travel advocates, and passionate storytellers — all united by one belief: that the most transformative journeys happen when you travel with knowledge, respect, and genuine curiosity.
